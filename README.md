### Alex Voss

I am strategic product leader and enterprise solutions specialist for [Zensical]. The [amazing Zensical team] develops a fresh take on what tooling for technical writing should look like. We combine a long-term strategic approach with close collaboration with our professional users in [Zensical Spark] and rapid iteration from the exploration of the problem space to providing solutions that deliver value to our users today, tomorrow, and in the future.
